Loan Terms & Conditions

Interest Rates

MZAZI TECH ENTERPRISE charges high-risk premium rates: 15% monthly, 35% quarterly, or 75% annually. Rates are non-negotiable and disclosed upfront.

Eligibility

Applicants must be 18+ years old with a verifiable income source. All applications are subject to verification.

Disbursement

Approved funds are disbursed within 24 hours to your registered M-Pesa number or bank account.

Repayment

Repayments must be made on or before the due date. Late payments incur a 5% penalty fee per week of delay.

Default

Failure to repay will result in reporting to credit bureaus, legal action, and recovery through registered guarantors or assets where applicable.

Privacy Policy

Your data is collected for loan processing and identity verification only. We do not sell data to third parties. All information is protected with AES-256 encryption. For data requests, email privacy@mzazitech.com.
